An appeal to truth

Some of the TV shows and movies that we watch convey an ideology called Solipsism, it is the view or idea that the self is all that can be known to exist. For many years now I somehow held this ideology myself even though I knew that there was absolute truth in existence. I wondered how I could have held such extremely opposing views for so long? I even remember arguing with someone on-line that while I agree the world is based on perception there are certain processes that are absolute, like the idea that the universe was created by some kind of intelligence. If everything that happens in this world is all in our heads then is there any real reason to make this world a better place to live? If we don't come together on solving the problems of of the world will it be OK that our world is destroyed by designing people who are apart of false institutions that say they have our best interest at heart as they act against the common good? Can we create real change if we are being convinced to take sides not of our choosing on important issues?

I want to hold a event on the square downtown called the "Truth Fair" on the weekend before the 4th of July on Sunday July 2nd from 1pm to 5pm that focuses on educating and talking about solving the problems. I am saving money for that very purpose now.

Lets come together on the most basic levels:

  1. What are the most important problems we face and what or who is responsible.
  2. Come to our own understandings about these topics from the grass roots level.
  3. Lets stop listening to people in political parties, or religions when they tell us what the problems are and how to fix them.

I need support, please visit my website at http://www.commonwealths.org to find out more. The Commonwealths website is a place I store information about ideals for a building a better society.
