Dean Clifford Releases his first Coop Stewardship Initative

Submitted by Scott Larson on Thu, 09/10/2015 - 12:31am

Dean Clifford, freedom and truth practitioner releases his first stewardship initiative called earthsc.com aimed at organizing a cooperative of individuals who actually want to do something to change the world . The site so far has a signup and donation request of $15.00 a month. The point is to have members contribute money toward actual solutions:

  • 100% funds raised go back into the cooperative and its related projects which progress is shown.

Based on the video, Dean's position is that for all the people protesting government or complaining about all the bad stuff in the world, what if 25,000 people were to put $15.00 toward an alternative community projects that improve the living conditions of the people? It sounds like he wants to build something like a living space where people can live and grow food, along with some alternative energy solutions.


He has a point, What if we started focusing on an 80/20 rule where we focus on acting on 80% of what is positive in the world, and 20% of what needs improvement? What kind of world would we live in?
