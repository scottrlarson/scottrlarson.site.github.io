Ideas and Concepts for Forming a Strong Community With a Presentation on a Decentralization Proposal: The Commonwealths

Date: 
Saturday, May 6, 2017 - 10:30am to 12:30pm

Please join us for the second meeting on building a stronger community. To register for this event, please sign in or Create a Commonwealths Account. This event can be found under the Events Link at the top menu of the site.

Introduction

Both Scott and Gary are working on ideas to help us build a stronger community. We will be discussing some ways we can do this with the input of all who have an interest in bringing real change free from outside centralized governing principals. We want people to join us in building a better world for everyone to live in free from control of self-interests.

Scott plans to answer questions about the Commonwealths Proposal, a framework to replacing centralized government with decentralized local communities working together to operate society. Scott plans to talk about how he envisions the structure,  working to invite members, and how we can take some of the principals from the Commonwealths and use them in building Community and Solidarity here in Santa Rosa and the surrounding area. The Commonwealths proposal is a goal for an organized self-governing society that has a few prerequisites that need to be met before it can be taken to the next level.

There are two compoents that will be addressed at this Gathering:

  1. Building a strong community gathering to talk about and propose ideas though film, writing, or some kind of media to strengthen our understanding of what is holding us back from creating a world each of us want to live in. We are currently calling this the "Community-Solidary Group".
  2. Scott will be talking about Roman history and how Roman concepts in law and government are used to circumvent peoples rights.
  3. Scott will explain the reasons for forming a well structured an organized group and why this organization needs to be assembled to combat this threat
  4. Scott will be explaining the need for a common group to be assembled. His idea, called The Commonwealths Proposal will be explaining the plan that aims to support people in there community building efforts. Its frame work for building a decentralized society and the need to free the people from centralized control systems in our current society today.

 

The Gathering Outline:

  • Commonwealths Proposal Presentation: What is the Commonwealths: Scott explains what the proposal is and what state it is at and the prerequisites are for moving forward.
  • Scott is also going to bring some materials for review on advanced concepts of working with crowds and dealing with dictatorships in a democracy: "From Dictatorship to Democracy" by Gene Sharp and "The Crowd: A Studio of the Popular Mind" By Gustave Le Bon.
  • Scott and Gary will be working on a draft for an email communication to introduce people into the commonwealths concepts.
  • Discussion about social networking platforms: which ones to use if any, and the privacy, security and independent control ramifications of using 3rd party for-profit platforms.
  • Concepts on social conditioning and how to protect ourselves from it. Scott will be bringing material on the subject and is interested in feedback on the topic: How to Reclaim your Mind and Life from the Cultural Engineers.
  • How Love Stopped The Cell Tower Next Door: How love and not frustration or anger brought real change to a small town.

 

We will be meeting at an undisclosed location in the Republic of Santa Rosa, California. If you wish to attend, register for the event and the details for the location will be emailed to you the day before the event.

If you have any questions please contact Scott Larson by logging into the site and sending a message.
