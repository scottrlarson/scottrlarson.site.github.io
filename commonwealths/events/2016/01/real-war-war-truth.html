Self-Organized Community Gathering (Talkshoe)

Date: 
Sunday, February 7, 2016 - 2:00pm to 5:15pm

Our first talkshoe on the steps needed to be taken to put together a self-organized society, and to bring awareness to our local communities to promote decentralization as a alternative to central government and how we can lessen the impact of centralization by bring awareness to corruption.

We plan to hold a our first community gathering event on Talkshoe TalkShoe is a website for user-generated live and recorded voice content. Users can host and join live voice discussion groups, radio talk show, conversations, and talk shows, called Community Call, using telephones, cell phones or Internet voice (voice-over-IP, VoIP). All Community Calls can be recorded for listening, downloading or podcasting to an iPod. Host make money for hosting discussion groups, talk shows, friends and family chats, book clubs, hobbyists' meetings, political debates, and education and training.

The goals of the gathering are listed on the Operational Plan. If you are interested in joining this group, to find out more please sign-up. The group wont actually start until we have 7 members. So I might postpone it if that happens. If you know a person that is interested in acting to make the world a better place please invite him. If you plan on attending this event, please be sure to sign-up for talkshoe before the event and register for this event by first signing up for an account at the commonwealths, then revisiting this page and click on the register tab.

We probably wont start until 3pm to give everyone enough time to get talkshoe going, solve any technical issues that they might have with the platform. So if you have already signed up for talkshoe and have tested being able to talk on a broadcast you could probably roll by 3pm if you dont like waiting for things to start. I think we are going to need about two hours for this gathering, not including the first hour for setup to get enough time for the intro, the outline and for everyone to share there thoughts an ideas. Im expecting at least 5 individuals, but the max 7 for now.

Outline

2:00pm: Dealing with technical issues, getting people into talkshoe, testing to make sure everything works.
3:00pm: Introducing Myself Scott, the community organizer and my wife, the switchboard operator and my oracle. she keeps me on track.
3:05pm: Before I start every gathering, I am always going to express a affirmation/declaration that protects this community from spiritual harm and aligns everything with the universal laws of nature.
3:10pm: Introduction to the first Self-Organized Society Gathering and the mission of a Self-Organized Society
3:20pm: Introductions: Spend 5mins or less introducing yourself by a name that you feel comfortable with, talk about what brought you to this gathering. What your strengths and weakness are.
4:00pm: Questions and Answers, Ideas and Suggestions.
4:50pm: Agreements on moving forward and plan of attack for on the ground activism.
5:00pm: Talk about individual qualities and see what tasks, if any need to be performed and fill those roles on a volunteer basis.
5:10pm: Closing Declaration: affirmation that we move forward in love and peace, free from fear and violence.
5:15pm: Official Gathering End Time for indivduals that need to leave.
5:20pm: After Hours Questions and Feedback If Any: if anybody has any thing they want to bring up at the end they can stay after to talk about it. The broadcast will be recorded so anybody can listen to it later if they'd like.
6:00pm: Scott, the community organizer goes to eat and play games.
