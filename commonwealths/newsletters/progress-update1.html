Submitted by Scott Larson on Thu, 03/26/2015 - 9:08pm

What is The Commonwealths?

The Commonwealths is a proposal for a cooperative approach to operating a trustworthy and balanced society. It's a set of communities containing Sovereign individuals who voluntarily participate in the operation of their own community, and when necessary cooperates between each non-local community within the Commonwealths at large for mutual benefit.

In the Commonwealths individual's and ideas that benefit the Commonwealths are tracked and accounted for by a set of principles called P.A.S.T (Participatory, Accountable, Skillful and Trustworthy) The goal is to use Web-based technologies to allow sovereign individual's who are apart of the Commonwealths to gain a feeling of trust that everyone in the community are held to P.A.S.T principles by vouching for, or making transparent Sovereign individuals and their actions within the Commonwealths though inner-connected websites that allow each individual rate or score a individual's actions within the Commonwealths.

What Stage is this Proposal at?

We are in the development stage. The main concept is there but we need to put it into practice by getting a working website with solid security that operates the P.A.S.T system. We can then promote the system to the public and run a test on the system. The idea is to run the system during the 2016 election debate, where the nominees playing the game of who can promise the most and do the least when they actually come into office. I'm currently Developing on the Commonwealths.org website. The site will be the home of the first Council of the Commonwealths and the Committee for Political Change.
