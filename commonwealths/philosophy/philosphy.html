Principles

Philosophical principals that the Commonwealths operate by. Every member of the Commonwealths is expected to learn and study these principals so that we can understand and operate by them. Please make yourself aware of them to minimize mis-communications or mis-understandings in the Commonwealths.

Also watch the documentary "Powers of Ten" to get an idea on the relationship between the universe and the physical world.
